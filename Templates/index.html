Introduction

  • Vyavsayi Vidhya Pratishthan is established by Rajkot Nagrik Sahakari Bank Ltd to promote excellence in engineering education.The trust was founded in 1996 with an objective of meeting technical requirements of Gujarat. V.V.P Engineering college has been matching ahead with a vision of transforming aspiring engineers to industry ready engineers, serving the nation at large. The disciplined academic environment strengthening technical foundation of the students,is the unique identity of V.V.P Innovation, creativity, team-spirit, entrepreneurship add to the scholastic achievements of the students and give them opportunities to enhance their personalities. The transparent governance and dedicated teaching by the qualified faculties make V.V.P. one of the most sought for engineering colleges of Gujarat.
